This contacts importer script is integrating with content management systems (aka CMS) like Buddy Zone, JamRoom, myBB, Joomla, symfony, SimpleMachines Forum (SMF), phpFoX, PhpBB, PHPMELODY, Dating Pro, jamit job, Boonex Dolphin, Wordpress, Social Engine, joovili, vBulletin, PunBB, Drupal, nowFire, Joomla1.0, phpizabi. Open Inviter is written in PHP 5 (no database required but cURL or wget required) and running on any webserver (tested on Apache) offering advanced tell a friend features.
